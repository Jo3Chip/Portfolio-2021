Petmania



Design and Development of a new site for a local pet supplie sbusiness with the ability to maintain themselves.



Utilising Figma to design the site and Wordpress to Develop it I was able to give the client a modern site that can be updated regularly and easily by themselves.The customer requested I use an existing theme so I chose Astra for this site due it’s fast and lightweight design. I also created a handbook that went through all the instructions they would need to update the site and used a plug-in to create a custom backend UI that simplified the experience further. All of this led to a successful site that can be expanded in the future with facilities such as e-commerce.
